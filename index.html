What we're building

One platform for every trip.

From the spark of an idea to the last boarding pass, FlyAll pulls the chaos of travel into a single, calm workflow that adapts to how you actually travel.

Intelligent booking

Search, compare and book flights, stays and experiences without the twenty tabs. A single unified flow, end to end.

Living itineraries

Every reservation, confirmation and detail organised automatically — and rescheduled the moment anything changes.

Personal to you

Recommendations shaped by how you travel — not by sponsored placement. The more you fly, the sharper it gets.

Real-time alerts

Delays, gate changes, visa updates, weather impacts — surfaced the moment they matter, never a minute later.

Built to travel with

Anywhere you go

About FlyAll

A calmer way to move through the world.

Travel technology hasn't meaningfully changed in a decade. Booking still sprawls across countless tabs. Confirmations still live in thirty different inboxes. Trip planning still feels like a second job.

FlyAll is a small team — based in Tel Aviv, Israel — rebuilding the travel stack from the traveler out. Every workflow that used to cost you an evening should take a minute. Every surprise should be a good one.
